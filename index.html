EV Chargers Buffer in Irvine, California

This interactive map visualizes EV charging access relative to office locations using OpenStreetMap data via OSMnx. Due to computational constraints of generating walking isochrones (5, 10, 15 min) for all of Orange County, the analysis focuses on Irvine, a key employment corridor.

The map includes network-based walking buffers, 5-minute charging catchments, and point layers for EV chargers and office locations.
